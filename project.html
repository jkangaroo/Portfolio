Farm Composting

DSGN 384: Interdisciplinary Design Projects

challenge

Design a suitable composting unit for Wagner Farm, and possibly other mid-sized farms to facilitate composting of manure and organic waste.

solution and features

Working in a team of five, we determined that the aerated static pile system (ASP) was most appropriate method of composting for Wagner Farm. This is space efficient, low-cost, and most importantly, it produces compost quicker and with better quality. In our system, fans induce air flow through a composting pile by sucking in air through perforated pipes below the pile.

process

  • interviews with experts
  • user observation and interview
  • farm visits
  • prototyping
  • minimal viable product testing
